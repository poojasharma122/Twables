Corporate Accommodation, Simplified

Manage every hotel stay in one place—built for Canadian businesses that need clarity, control, and flexibility.

Twables helps your team book trusted accommodations while giving your company full visibility into spend, policies, and travel behavior. No more scattered bookings, surprise expenses, or lost invoices. With Twables, corporate lodging becomes a breeze.

Hotel Booking That Works for Everyone

Access top hotel rates across Canada and beyond

From downtown Toronto to conferences in Calgary, book from a wide network of trusted hotels, lodges, and executive suites. Get exclusive corporate pricing and negotiated rates without the back-and-forth.

Company policies built in

Ensure every reservation fits your travel policy. You control the rules—set nightly limits, preferred vendors, and approval flows—while employees book directly within those guidelines.

Last-minute flexibility

Plans change. Twables offers flexible booking options, including free cancellations and no-penalty modifications on select accommodations. Changes can be made instantly online—no need to call customer service.

Invoices you can actually use

Stop tracking down receipts from different hotels. Twables consolidates all bookings into a single CAD invoice, categorized by traveler or department.

CRA-compliant documentation

Download detailed, tax-ready receipts for expense reporting. Easily export summaries to share with finance or integrate directly with your accounting tools.

Real-time reporting

Track lodging spend by employee, city, or trip. Uncover trends, control costs, and make informed decisions with accurate, up-to-date data.

Canadian Support, Wherever You Stay

Whether it’s a single overnight trip or a company-wide conference, Twables offers hands-on assistance from a support team that understands Canadian business travel. Available by phone or email whenever you need help—no bots, no delays.
